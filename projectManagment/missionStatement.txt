This is a Game.
User will be presented with an NxN grid of colored squares;
When the user clicks a square, it will be destroyed,
Any neighboring square of the same color will also be destoryed.
Each of those squares will also be destroyed, and so on.

Once all connecting squares have been destroyed, The remaing squares will drop to fill any empty spaces.
All remaing empty squares at the top will be filled with new random colors.


SCORING: Each color will be worth a numeric value, a move score will be the product of the number of squares destoryed in a chain and the square value;
